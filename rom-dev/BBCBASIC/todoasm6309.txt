Known Issues with 6309 assembler
================================

Incorrectly encoded instructions
 - DIVD 
 - DIVQ
 - LDQ


Missing instructions:
 - pshsw
 - pshuw
 - pulsw
 - puluw
 - tfm


Missing addressing modes:
 - E,ix
 - F,ix
 - W,ix
 - [E,ix]
 - [F,ix]
 - [W,ix]

Not tested:
 - BAND
 - BEOR
 - BIAND
 - BIEOR
 - BIO
 - BOR
 - LDBT
 - STBT

Desirable?
 - Little endian EQUD/EQUW


 Fixed
 =====

 2022/7/9

 - EQUW endianness
 - EQUD endianness
 - reg,reg instructions to/from "0" was encoded wrongly
